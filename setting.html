Privacy & Compliance

Good practices for responsible bulk email sending.

All member data and settings are stored only in this browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any external server by MailPilot itself — your SMTP credentials are used directly to connect to your chosen mail provider.

Sending Compliance

Always include unsubscribe linkAppend an unsubscribe link to every footer automatically
Require consent confirmationMark members as opted-in before they're eligible for broadcasts
Only email people who have agreed to receive communications from you. Most countries have anti-spam laws (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, etc.) — check what applies to you before sending bulk email.
